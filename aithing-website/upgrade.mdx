You can upgrade [AI Thing](https://aithing.dev) in two ways:

- **Seamless** – update from inside the app (recommended)
- **Non-seamless** – manually reinstall the app

<Tip>Your conversation history, API keys, and settings are all **stored locally and automatically restored** after an upgrade.</Tip>

---

## Seamless upgrade (recommended)

1. Open **AI Thing**.
2. Go to **Settings → Check for updates**.
3. If an update is available, click **Install update**.
4. Follow the on-screen prompts until the update completes.

---

## Non-seamless upgrade (manual reinstall)

Use this method if the seamless upgrade doesn’t work for you.

### 1. Uninstall the current version

1. Quit [AI Thing](https://aithing.dev) by pressing **Command + Q**
2. Open the **Applications** folder and drag **AI Thing** to the **Trash**.
